Frequently Asked Questions

What is Diplomaq.lol?

Diplomaq.lol is an online platform dedicated to enhancing debate, Model United Nations (MUN), and learning skills. We offer a variety of resources, including debates, learning modules, and personalized coaching.

How do I choose the right subscription plan?

Consider your needs and goals. The Free plan is great for exploring the platform. The Pro plan offers enhanced learning. The Elite plan provides personalized coaching. The Institutional plan is for schools and organizations.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?

Yes, you can easily upgrade or downgrade your subscription at any time through your account settings.
